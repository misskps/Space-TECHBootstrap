Some of the asked Questions and Answers?

Which course should someone who wants to pursue astronomy study? One needs to take science during their 10+2 to pursue an astronomy course in further studies. For undergraduate in astronomy and masters and PhD in astronomy, one needs to have a degree in science stream in Subjects of maths, physics and astrophysics or astronomy.

Will I get job opportunities in NASA or ISRO with an astronomy degree? If an individual pursuing a degree in astronomy has relevant skills and expertise in the course, he or she may have chances to get employed at NASA or ISRO. However, it depends on the individual’s skills and dedication towards the subject since they are prestigious companies.

What is the career scope of a degree in astronomy? Astronomy offers a limited scope since the recruitments are based on highly skilled individuals in the subject matter. However, one can work in the fields of astronomy as an astronomer, research analyst, observation astronomer, and much more.
